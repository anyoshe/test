Who are WE

Bonariza classic is milk distribution company based in Malindi, Kilifi county. We are know for distributing Milk and its products and eggs. We are proud to make our customers happy by ensuring they get our products at their doorsteps.

We at Bonariza we work with specific routes consistently and also supply our products on calls. We have return policy which helps our customers not to incur losses with our products since they are perishable goods.
